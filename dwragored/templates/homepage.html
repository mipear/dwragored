About Dŵr Agored

Are you a cold water swimmer based in South Wales? Dŵr Agored (Open Water) is a resource for cold water swimmers to share and browse swims, using the All Swims page. As a cold water swimmer myself, I know sometimes it is hard to find the right location. Navigate to the Location page to add a new location, then "Add A Swim" on the All Swims page. Record your swim and share your experiences to enrich the lives of others.

Find a recommended swim and embrace the benefits:
  • Cost effective - once you have the gear, it’s free!
  • Boosts your immune system by increasing the production of white blood cells
  • Improves blood circulation by constricting your blood vessels which helps in oxygenating your body
  • Reduces stress - it lowers your blood pressure and improves your heart health
  • Great way to meet people - chill and chatter
